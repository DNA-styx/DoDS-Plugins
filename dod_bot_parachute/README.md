# dod_bot_parachute

Sourcemod plugin that detects a bot is falling, will press the bot's +USE key to activate their parachute until they are detected as being on teh ground (if installed on the server).    

Tested with sm_parachute version 2.5 https://forums.alliedmods.net/showthread.php?p=580269
